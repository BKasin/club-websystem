Association for Computing Machinery Club is a club at CSU, San Bernardino devoted to computer science, computer engineering, and computer systems. Interested in us? Feel free to checkout our Facebook Group or GitHub account. We also have a page on the CSE Department website. The club office is in Jack Brown room 540.

Membership Application

Our club is open to everyone, and we encourage you to join! You can do so by filling out the membership application and either turning it into an officer, or dropping it off in our office. We do have a membership fee of $10 per quarter. At this time we can only accept either cash or check.

Hangouts

Every week, we host a hangout on TeamSpeak where you can come and collaborate with fellow members and discuss interesting topics.

Day Time Server
Sunday 6pm - 9pm acm-csusb.org

TeamSpeak Instructions

  1. Download and install TeamSpeak 3 Client from their download page.
  2. Launch TeamSpeak 3 and go under "Bookmarks" >> "Manage Bookmarks" then click "Add Bookmark"
  3. Fill in the following information under the respected fields:
    • Label: ACM Club
    • Address: acm-csusb.org
    • Nickname: (Alias we may refer to you by)
  4. Hit "Apply" then "Ok"
  5. Select "Bookmartks" >> "ACM Club"
  6. Welcome To The Club's TeamSpeak Server!!

Projects

As a member of the ACM Club, you are able to participate or lead any of the club projects we may have. Participating in these projects is a great way to broaden and improve your abilities in our field. One of the most important things that you can have on your resume is a portfolio of work that you have done outside of university classes.

For more information on each of our individual projects, see our projects page.
